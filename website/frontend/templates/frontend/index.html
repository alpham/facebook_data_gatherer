What's Arabic Social Media Sentiment Analysis

we now have a huge volume of opinionated arabic data in the social media on the Web. Arabic Social Media Sentiment Analysis is focuses on opinions which express positive or negative sentiments. Helping you with simple and awesome visualization for data analysis.

Our Software Features

Our software has many features which help you understand your data fast and simply.

Using more than one classifier gives you good accuracy and low error rate for you output.

Visulaized Results give you good , fast and easy understand for your output.

More of Features

There are more of features for our software which related with the type and form of the input data.

Our Software can deal with all forms of Arabic Writing like Modern Standard Arabic(MSA),Dialectal Arabic(DA) and Franco-Arabic.

You can enter your inpute by many ways:

  • insert sentence manually and classify it.
  • upload data as txt file.
  • collect data from facebook page OR specify post Link.
